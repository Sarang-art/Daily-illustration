About

Daily Illustration was created by Sarang, a designer and illustrator from Rio de Janeiro, Brazil. This project is for artists and art lovers—to help them discover new creators and find tools that sharpen their skills (and their passion). I’ve always loved art, but I also love organizing things.
Artists deserve to focus on creating, not wasting time digging through scattered resources. Whenever I studied or looked for inspiration, I noticed how everything was spread all over the internet. That’s why I built this: one place for useful tools, articles, and curated art. It started as a portfolio piece, but it took on a life of its own. Even though it’s run by just me, I want to keep it free and accessible. If you enjoy what you find here and want to support the project, donations are always appreciated!
